Fried Rice

Even kids will love this quick and easy fried rice, perfect for a weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white long-grain
  • 2 tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs, lightly whisked
  • 2 bacon rashers, chopped
  • 1 carrot, peeled and grated
  • 2 shallots, trimmed, finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook rice in boiling water for 12 minutes
  2. Heat oil and cook scrambled eggs
  3. Heat olive oil and cook ground beef
  4. Add bacon to wok and cook until golden
  5. Add remaining ingredients and stir for 3-4 minutes.
